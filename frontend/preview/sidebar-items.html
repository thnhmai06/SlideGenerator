Studio navigation pattern — topbar tabs + subtabs (no sidebar)

Topbar — main tabs (Recipes · Studio)

Pill switcher. Animated sliding indicator under .tab-indicator. Active tab gets full-colour foreground + 100% icon opacity; idle tabs are --muted-foreground with 55% icon opacity. Sits centered in the topbar between brand logo (left) and theme switch + utility icons (right).

Subtabs — Studio inner pages

Underline subtabs. The 3px bar under the active tab scales in over 220ms. Status dot (badge-dot) appears next to Đang chạy while a workflow is active (pulsing) and next to Kết quả after completion (steady). Use only one level of subtabs — Studio is intentionally flat.

Why no sidebar

Earlier drafts used a left-pinned sidebar (Create Task · Process · Results · Settings · About). The Studio surface compresses that into a single horizontal axis: brand → tab pill → utility cluster. Settings + About live as icon-btn in the topbar-right cluster, not as nav entries. The sidebar pattern is deprecated; do not introduce new sidebar items.
